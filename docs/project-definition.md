# AI Manager for Nano–Micro Influencers

## 1. Project Overview

This project aims to build an **AI Manager** for nano–micro influencers (≤ 10,000 subscribers) that assists with the **entire content production decision flow** — from topic ideation to script writing and thumbnail creation.

The system provides fast, data-driven, and channel-specific recommendations so that creators can focus on execution rather than spending excessive time on planning and research.

---

## 2. Target Users

- YouTube creators with **under 10,000 subscribers**
- Early-stage creators who:
  - Lack structured content planning workflows
  - Spend significant time choosing topics
  - Struggle to differentiate their scripts from competitors
  - Do not have design or marketing expertise for thumbnails

---

## 3. Core Problem

Small creators typically face:

- High time cost in discovering **viable and trendy topics**
- Writing scripts that are:
  - Too generic
  - Similar to competitors
  - Lacking strong evidence or insight
- Difficulty designing thumbnails that:
  - Match the content
  - Attract clicks
  - Align with their channel identity

---

## 4. Project Goal

To function as a **virtual content manager** that:

> Helps creators consistently produce competitive, differentiated, and trend-aligned YouTube content with minimal planning overhead.

---

## 5. Key Objectives

### Topic Discovery
- Recommend **trending topics** optimized for:
  - The user’s channel theme
  - Past content style
  - Target audience
  - Current market trends

### Script Generation
- Generate scripts that:
  - Are not generic
  - Are differentiated from competing videos
  - Include:
    - Competitive video analysis
    - Unique insights
    - Data-driven arguments
    - Supporting evidence from news articles and reliable sources

### Thumbnail Recommendation
- Suggest thumbnail concepts aligned with:
  - The script narrative
  - Emotional hook
  - Click-through optimization
  - Channel branding

### Growth Support
- Assist users in:
  - Increasing subscribers
  - Improving engagement
  - Raising probability of appearing in **Trending / Recommended sections**

---

## 6. User Benefits

### 1) Time Efficiency
- Reduce topic research time from hours to minutes
- Accelerate script writing significantly

### 2) Content Quality
- Produce insight-driven scripts instead of surface-level summaries
- Compete with larger creators via strategic positioning

### 3) Channel Consistency
- Maintain a consistent tone, format, and brand identity

### 4) Growth Acceleration
- Increase:
  - Click-through rate (CTR)
  - Watch time
  - Subscriber conversion

---

## 7. Functional Scope (Initial)

### Input
- Channel metadata
- Past video titles
- Target audience
- Preferred tone/style
- Keywords or rough topic ideas (optional)

### Output
- Ranked topic recommendations
- Script draft + references
- Thumbnail concept suggestions (visual + textual)

---

## 8. Long-Term Vision

To evolve from a simple assistant into:

> A fully autonomous AI content manager that continuously analyzes the market, competitors, and user performance metrics to refine strategy and maximize creator growth.

---

## 9. Success Metrics

- Reduction in planning time per video
- Improvement in:
  - Average views
  - CTR
  - Watch time
  - Subscriber growth rate
- User retention and repeated usage

---

## 10. Positioning Statement

> An AI Manager designed specifically for small creators — providing enterprise-level content strategy intelligence without enterprise-level complexity or cost.
